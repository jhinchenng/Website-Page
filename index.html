About Nintendo

Nintendo forged their path since 1889, when they began making hanafuda playing cards in Kyoto, Japan. Today, they're fortunate to be able to share their characters, ideas and worlds through the medium of video games and the entertainment industry.

Popular Games

Super Mario Odyssey

Join Mario on a globe-trotting adventure in Super Mario Odyssey! Explore vibrant worlds, collect Power Moons, and rescue Princess Peach from Bowser's clutches.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Experience a vast open world in this critically acclaimed game. Discover the ruins of Hyrule, solve puzzles, and battle foes as Link to save Princess Zelda.

Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Create your own paradise on a deserted island. Build, decorate, and interact with adorable animal villagers in this charming social simulation game.

Splatoon 3

Join the ink-splatting action in this colorful shooter! Compete in turf wars, customize your character, and enjoy a fun single-player campaign.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

The ultimate crossover fighting game featuring characters from various franchises. Battle your friends or the computer in epic matchups.
